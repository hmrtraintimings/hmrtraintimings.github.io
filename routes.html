PHASES


The Metro Project is proposed of 2 phases Phase I and Phase II


Phase I consinsts of 3 lines covering a total distance of 66.56 Km.


  • Line 1: Miyapur - LB Nagar - 29.21 km (18.15 miles) Red
  • Line 2: JBS - MGBS - 10.05 km (6.25 miles) Green
  • Line 3: Nagole - Shilparamam - 27.29 km (16.96 miles) Blue
